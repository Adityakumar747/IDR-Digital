Who We Are

About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles for regulated, high-consequence environments.

Through our unique partnership with leading universities and industry collaborators, IDR bridges academic insight with industry practice. Our practitioners work on real-world projects with financial services firms, critical infrastructure operators, and government agencies, equipped with frameworks including NIST, ISO 27001, NIS2, and emerging AI governance methodologies.
